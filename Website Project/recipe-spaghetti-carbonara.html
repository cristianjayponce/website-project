Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta or Guanciale, diced 150g
Eggs 4 large
Parmesan Cheese, grated 1 cup
Freshly Ground Black Pepper to taste
Salt to taste
Garlic, minced (optional) 2 cloves
Olive Oil 1 tablespoon

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente according to package instructions.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese until well combined. Season with a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the pancetta or guanciale and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes. If using, add minced garlic and saut√© for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  4. Once the spaghetti is cooked, reserve about 1 cup of the pasta water and then drain the spaghetti.
  5. Immediately add the hot spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss to combine, ensuring the spaghetti is well coated with the rendered fat.
  6. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing continuously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
  7. Season with additional black pepper and salt to taste.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with extra grated Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of black pepper.
