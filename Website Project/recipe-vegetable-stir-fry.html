Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Broccoli Florets 1 cup
Bell Peppers, sliced 1 cup
Carrots, julienned 1/2 cup
Snow Peas 1/2 cup
Garlic, minced 2 cloves
Ginger, grated 1 teaspoon
Soy Sauce 3 tablespoons
Oyster Sauce 2 tablespoons
Sesame Oil 1 tablespoon
Vegetable Oil 2 tablespoons
Honey 1 tablespoon
Red Chili Flakes 1/2 teaspoon
Green Onions, chopped 2 tablespoons
Sesame Seeds 1 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. Prepare all vegetables by washing and cutting them into desired sizes.
  2. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, and red chili flakes to create the stir-fry sauce.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, saut√© until fragrant.
  5. Add carrots and bell peppers, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add broccoli florets and snow peas, continue to stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  7. Pour in the prepared stir-fry sauce and toss to coat all the vegetables evenly.
  8. Drizzle sesame oil over the vegetables and give a final toss.
  9. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.
