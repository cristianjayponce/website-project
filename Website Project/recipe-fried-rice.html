Fried Rice

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Cooked Rice (preferably day-old) 3 cups
Vegetable Oil 2 tablespoons
Garlic, minced 3 cloves
Onion, finely chopped 1 medium
Carrots, diced 1/2 cup
Frozen Peas 1/2 cup
Eggs, beaten 2 large
Soy Sauce 3 tablespoons
Oyster Sauce 2 tablespoons
Salt to taste
Black Pepper to taste
Green Onions, chopped (for garnish) 2 tablespoons
Protein of Choice (optional, e.g., shrimp, chicken, pork) 1 cup

Instructions

  1. **Prepare Ingredients**: Ensure all vegetables are washed, peeled, and chopped into small, uniform pieces. If adding protein, cook it separately and set aside.
  2. **Heat Oil**: In a large wok or skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. **Sauté Aromatics**: Add the minced garlic and chopped onion to the wok. Sauté until the onions become translucent and the garlic is fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. **Cook Vegetables**: Add the diced carrots and frozen peas to the wok. Stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. **Scramble Eggs**: Push the vegetables to one side of the wok. Pour the beaten eggs into the empty side and scramble until fully cooked.
  6. **Add Rice**: Break up any clumps in the cooked rice and add it to the wok. Mix everything together, ensuring the rice is evenly distributed with the vegetables and eggs.
  7. **Season**: Pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir-fry the mixture for another 5-7 minutes, allowing the sauces to coat the rice evenly. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. **Add Protein**: If using any pre-cooked protein (like shrimp, chicken, or pork), add it to the wok and stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat through.
  9. **Garnish and Serve**: Remove the fried rice from heat. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
  10. **Enjoy**: Serve hot as a main dish or as a side to your favorite Asian-inspired meals.
