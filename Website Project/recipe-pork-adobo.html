Pork Adobo

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pork Belly, cut into cubes 1 kg
Vinegar 1/2 cup
Soy Sauce 1/2 cup
Water 1 cup
Garlic, minced 6 cloves
Bay Leaves 3 leaves
Whole Black Peppercorns 1 teaspoon
Brown Sugar 2 tablespoons
Salt to taste
Oil 2 tablespoons
Green Onions, chopped (for garnish) 2 tablespoons
Hard-Boiled Eggs (optional) 4 pcs

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork belly and cook until it starts to brown.
  3. Pour in the vinegar and let it boil without stirring for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the soy sauce, water, bay leaves, and whole peppercorns. Stir to combine.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer covered for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the pork is tender.
  6. Stir in the brown sugar and season with salt to taste. Continue to simmer uncovered for an additional 15 minutes to reduce the sauce to your desired consistency.
  7. If using hard-boiled eggs, add them to the pot during the last 10 minutes of cooking to allow them to absorb the flavors.
  8. Remove the bay leaves and peppercorns before serving.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot with steamed rice.
