Chicken Teriyaki

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken Thighs 500g
Soy Sauce 1/4 cup
Mirin 1/4 cup
Sake 2 tablespoons
Brown Sugar 2 tablespoons
Garlic, minced 2 cloves
Ginger, grated 1 teaspoon
Sesame Oil 1 tablespoon
Green Onions, chopped 2 tablespoons
Sesame Seeds 1 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, mirin, sake, brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger to make the teriyaki sauce.
  2. Marinate the chicken thighs in the sauce for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat sesame oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the marinated chicken thighs and cook until browned on both sides.
  5. Pour the remaining marinade into the pan and simmer until the sauce thickens and the chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds before serving.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice and vegetables.
